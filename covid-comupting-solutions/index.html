Genomics is the study of how different allele in a genome effects that species' properties and understanding of gene sequences, their functions, gene regulation, and the impact of genetic variations.
We can model how the DNA model looks like using machine learning algorithms such as positive-unlabeled machine learning.

Epidemiological Models for Covid-19

Epidemiological models are computer modules that predict where the virus will be spreading in the future depending on current data.
Epidemiological models have been very useful to make decisions such as where to direct medical attention where to isolate by putting into a quarantine effect.
These are very hard to model mostly due to unpredictable nature of human behaviour and chaos of natural events such as the wind. But using machine learning and great data set collected online and governments, computer scientists were able to predict the spread of the virus and possible mutations it may go through.

Information Access

Of course it was crucial to stay insite during Covid-19 quarantines.
But students needed education, people needed indoor entertainments, and everyone was concerned about the state of the world and their surrounding, so they wanted live information.
Fortunately many resources have been made available to the public.

  • Almost every school capable of internet access moved to online education. This was mostly via online meeting platforms such as Zoom or Microsoft Teams.
  • Many young people kept up their friendships through online games and online calls through Discord.
  • The government provided online access to live updates on Covid-19 reports.
